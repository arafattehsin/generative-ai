# Procurement Policy Extract

Use this extract when assessing the vendor quotes.

## Mandatory Checks

- Vendor must disclose warranty duration and support service level.
- Vendor must provide either ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, or an equivalent security attestation.
- Any bid above the approved budget ceiling requires CFO approval.
- Any bid with delivery after the required date must include a written mitigation plan.
- The selected vendor must provide itemized pricing before purchase order release.
- Any replacement-device buffer below 5 percent requires operational risk sign-off.

## Approval Guidance

- Procurement can recommend a vendor below the approved budget ceiling if mandatory checks are met.
- Procurement can recommend a vendor above the budget ceiling only when schedule or risk benefits are explicit and CFO approval is listed as a dependency.
- Warranty exceptions must be negotiated before purchase order release.
- Delivery staging by region is strongly preferred for field operations.

## Output Standard

The recommendation must include:

- selected vendor
- decision rationale
- weighted score table
- policy risks
- negotiation points
- purchase order readiness decision
