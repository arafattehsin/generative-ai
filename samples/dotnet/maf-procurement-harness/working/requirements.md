# Managed Laptop Refresh Requirements

Contoso Retail Operations needs to refresh laptops for store managers, regional operations leads, and the field merchandising team.

## Scope

- 180 business laptops
- Docking support for 70 regional users
- Standard image support for Windows 11 Enterprise
- Three-year warranty and next-business-day replacement target
- Delivery required before 2026-08-30
- Rollout window: 2026-09-02 to 2026-09-20

## Mandatory Requirements

- TPM 2.0 and Secure Boot support
- Microsoft Entra ID join and Intune enrollment compatibility
- Minimum 16 GB RAM and 512 GB SSD
- Vendor must provide device serial export before shipment
- Vendor must provide a named implementation contact
- Vendor must support Australia-wide delivery

## Preferred Requirements

- Carbon impact reporting per shipment
- Packaging take-back or recycling option
- At least 10 percent hot-swap buffer available during rollout
- Ability to stage deliveries by region
- Clear escalation path for warranty exceptions

## Budget Guidance

The approved budget ceiling is AUD 315,000 excluding GST. Lower cost is useful, but delivery confidence and policy compliance should not be sacrificed for the cheapest price.
