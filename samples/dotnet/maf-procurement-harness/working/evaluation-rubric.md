# Evaluation Rubric

Score each vendor from 1 to 5 for each category, then multiply by the weight.

| Category | Weight | What Good Looks Like |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Commercial value | 30 | Competitive total cost, clear itemization, no hidden services |
| Delivery confidence | 20 | Delivery before 2026-08-30 with credible staging and named contact |
| Security and compliance | 20 | ISO 27001 or SOC 2 Type II, device management compatibility, clean policy fit |
| Support and warranty | 15 | Three-year warranty, next-business-day replacement, clear escalation |
| Sustainability | 10 | Carbon reporting, packaging recycling, device take-back |
| Operational risk | 5 | Low rollout risk, adequate buffer, few approval dependencies |

Maximum weighted score: 500.

## Recommendation Rule

Recommend the highest-scoring vendor that satisfies mandatory policy checks. If the highest score has a policy exception, call it out and recommend the best policy-compliant path.
